Dads Spaghetti

This recipe is incredibly simple and should take no more than 25 minutes to make. Dads Spaghetti is an obvious poke at spaghetti made by dads. Not only is it simple and mostly bland, but you are supposed to slightly overcook the noodles before serving them. This is a funny dish and incredibly easy if you're looking to have a little fun.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 2 pounds of lean ground beef
  • 1 minced onion
  • 1 (6 ounce) can of tomato paste
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans of condensed tomato soup
  • 2 1/2 cups of water

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of slightly salted water to a boil
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes
  3. Drain water
  4. Cook ground beef with onion in a large skiller over medium heat until meat turns brown
  5. Return meat mixture to pan with tomato paste, tomato soup and water
  6. Cook for 5-15 minutes
