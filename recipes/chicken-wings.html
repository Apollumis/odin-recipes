Caramelized Chicken Wings

Yes, it is as delicious as it sounds. These fantastic wings are super easy to fix up and should be on everyone's bucket list. These caramelized wings practically melt in your mouth with flavour and texture. It is an easy preperation and only takes about an hour to an hour and a half to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 12 large chicken wings, tips removed and wings cut in half at joint
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds, or to taste

Steps

  1. Medium heat, mix together water, sugar, soy sauce, peanut butter, honey, wine vinegar, and garlic until smooth
  2. Place the wings into the sauce cover and let simmer for 30 minutes
  3. Uncover and simmer until wings are tender
  4. Occasionally spoon sauce over wings
  5. Sprinke with sesame seeds
