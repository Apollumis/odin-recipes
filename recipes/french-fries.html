Air Fryer French Fries

While they do sound awfully simple, these french fries will definitely make your bucket list. This recipe guarantees nice and crispy french fries that will go with any meal. They maintain a traditional taste, but only use a fraction of the ingredients. You will only need 4 ingredients to begin.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes
  2. Soak and drain the potatoes
  3. Season the French Fries
  4. Cook in Air Fryer for 10 mins
